Why Track?

Believe it or not, tracking your bowel movements can actually be a good thing. It helps you understand your body better, and can even lead to healthier habits. Plus, it's a lot more fun than you might think!

Have you ever wondered how long you spend on the toilet? How much water you use? How much toilet paper you go through? How much your job pays you for your time on the toilet?

LogLog is here to answer those questions and more.
